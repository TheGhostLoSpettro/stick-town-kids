Stick Town

An open-world stick-figure town adventure · Kids Edition
Splash! One day a friendly little stick figure tumbled out of the sky and landed right on Main Street in Stick Town! 🌟

Walk around town, say hi to the other stick folks, and step into any shop's door to go inside. Get strong at the gym, get smart at school, make friends, and earn shiny coins at work. Buy a bigger house and a faster ride to zoom down the street!

Adopt a pet pal 🐾 to follow you around, and hunt for the 5 shiny stars ⭐ hidden around town every single day!

Your big quest: become the Town Hero and win the Golden Trophy 🏆 from the Mayor!
